The sordid saga of Prince Andrew's association with Jeffrey Epstein is now further illuminated by the involvement of a shadowy figure named David Stern. This German businessman, largely unknown to the public, acted as a vital conduit between the disgraced royal and the convicted sex offender, primarily by exploiting his deep connections and understanding of China. The recently unsealed US Department of Justice documents reveal Stern's extensive role, suggesting he was instrumental in forging and maintaining these problematic links.

Stern, who presented himself as a discreet facilitator, operated for nearly a decade, making introductions, managing sensitive documents, and pitching business ventures for both Epstein and the Duke of York. His activities extended to assisting Sarah Ferguson with her significant debts, a task he seemingly undertook with Epstein's implicit guidance. The unearthed emails paint a disturbing picture of Stern's personal views, featuring numerous derogatory remarks about women, often employing crude abbreviations and reflecting a transactional, objectifying mindset. He himself admitted in a 2011 email to Epstein, "I stay in the background/hidden, just make the arrangements," a modus operandi that evidently persisted.

The documents show Stern's relationship with Epstein was deeply hierarchical, with Stern often addressing Epstein as his "mentor" or "general" and eagerly seeking his approval and guidance. This dynamic is particularly evident in their discussions about investment opportunities, often framed within the context of China's burgeoning economy, a region Stern claimed to have unparalleled access to. He reportedly boasted of friendships with influential figures in China, including associates of former President Jiang Zemin, adding to his perceived value.

Stern's introduction to the York family, according to the files, was orchestrated by Epstein. Emails from Sarah Ferguson in early 2010 indicate she met Stern at Royal Lodge for dinner after being introduced by Epstein, seeking his advice and recognizing his extensive network in China. Stern subsequently reached out to "Your Royal Highness," offering his assistance on unspecified matters. This period coincided with Ferguson's severe financial difficulties, with Stern and Epstein appearing to coordinate efforts to manage her debts, exhibiting a callous disrespect towards her in their private communications.

The article highlights how Stern, despite Prince Andrew's need to publicly distance himself from Epstein after the latter's 2008 conviction, continued to act as a go-between. Stern reportedly accompanied Andrew on official trips to China, leveraging his connections to facilitate meetings and ostensibly promote British interests. His integration into Andrew's orbit deepened with his appointment to the board of Pitch@Palace in 2016, the Prince's start-up competition. This position, along with his directorship at the royal charity St George's House, placed him in close proximity to the highest levels of the British establishment, despite lingering concerns about his background among some board members.

The connection to China, a nation often viewed with a mixture of opportunity and suspicion by Western powers, appears to have been the lynchpin of Stern's appeal. In the aftermath of the 2008 financial crisis, China's rapid economic growth presented a perceived goldmine for investors, a sentiment clearly shared by Epstein. Stern proposed setting up a London-based business discreetly focused on Chinese high-net-worth individuals, explicitly noting the intention to "use his 'aura and access'," referring to Prince Andrew.

The involvement of individuals like Stern underscores the complex web of relationships and financial dealings that characterized Epstein's network. His role as a facilitator, operating in the shadows and exploiting both royal connections and Chinese market access, serves as a stark reminder of how personal ambition and financial opportunism can intersect with, and potentially compromise, even the most privileged circles. Stern's persistent silence in the face of these revelations only adds to his enigmatic and, frankly, suspect persona.
