Darling, the influencer lifestyle is all fun and games until the Data Protection regulator decides to RSVP to your little promotional party. Waithera Imani, better known to her 200,000 Instagram followers as Cera Imani, has just been hit with a Sh50,000 bill for a privacy breach that is as messy as it is amateur. It seems our girl thought she could just plaster whatever she wanted on TikTok and Instagram without checking if she was stepping on a regular citizen's toes. 

The drama started when a certain Mr. Omwenga realized his phone number was being passed around like a cheap appetizer at an industry launch. He claims he never gave his digits to the eatery CJ’s, yet he was being bombarded with unsolicited texts after Cera’s promotional spree. It is the height of audacity, really. These brands think they can buy our data and our peace of mind for the price of a sponsored burger, and influencers like Cera are all too happy to be the middleman for a quick paycheck. 

This whole situation reminds me of the desperate attempts at relatability we see from international stars who think they are above the law. While Cera is busy leaking phone numbers, we have [Jessie Buckley’s Chanel-Scented Pity Party: Spanx, Breast Pumps, and the Art of Being Relatable](/posts/jessie-buckley-s-chanel-scented-pity-party--spanx--breast-pumps--and-the-art-of-being-relatable) proving that whether you are in Nairobi or London, the celebrity ego always thinks the rules do not apply to them. Cera has the numbers, sure, but she clearly lacks the common sense to realize that privacy is actually a thing in 2024. 

The court might have cleared a university student in a separate mess recently due to a lack of evidence, but Cera was not so lucky. Sh50,000 might be pocket change for someone with her "reach," but the reputation stain is much harder to wash out. Maybe next time, instead of worrying about the lighting for her TikTok, she should spend five minutes reading a data protection handbook. Stay messy, Cera, but maybe keep your hands off other people's personal info.
