So, Kisumu West detectives finally found the decomposing remains of a 21-year-old RIAT College student in Riat Forest. Apparently, she went missing on April 5th, after a night out with "colleagues" that went sideways. They say she was lured into a drinking spree, left incapacitated, assaulted, and dumped like garbage. Classic.

The prime suspect, one Steven Coleman Okoth, who’s allegedly her ex-boyfriend, has been nabbed. Police are pointing fingers at him for orchestrating the whole gruesome affair - the gang rape, the torture, and the eventual murder in Milimani. Her sister got a chilling text around midnight saying she'd been assaulted and left in a bush. Then, poof, her phone went dead. Standard procedure for these sorts of tragedies.

Detectives did their forensics, traced her last known location, and came up with zilch initially. But this Okoth guy, apparently, had been making threats for two months and stalking her. Funny how it often comes back to someone you already know, isn't it? They're still hunting for accomplices, which means this isn't a one-man show, but just another symptom of the rot. It's always more than just one person, isn't it?
