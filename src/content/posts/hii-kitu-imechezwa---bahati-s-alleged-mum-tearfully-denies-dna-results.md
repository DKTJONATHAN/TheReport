Bahati's Alleged Mum Cries Foul Over DNA Results: "This Thing Was Played"

This is what happens when you let the whole world into your family drama. Bahati, the musician who seems to have more drama than hits these days, had a DNA test done with Judith Makokha, who he calls his mum. Surprise, surprise, the results show they’re not related. Now she’s crying foul, claiming the whole thing was rigged. Classic.

Naturally, the woman is having a meltdown, denying the 0% probability that she’s his biological mother. She’s even accusing Bahati of bribing the hospital. Honestly, who has that kind of money to waste on proving they *aren't* someone's son? It’s all very dramatic, very public, and frankly, a bit sad. You'd think with all the noise about education and self-improvement these days, people would have better things to do than chase after these kinds of stories.

Bahati, ever the showman, is trying to play peacemaker, saying he believes her and will help her find her *real* child. Because that’s exactly what the world needs - another quest for a missing child fueled by a public paternity dispute. It’s like something out of those telenovelas we all pretend not to watch.
