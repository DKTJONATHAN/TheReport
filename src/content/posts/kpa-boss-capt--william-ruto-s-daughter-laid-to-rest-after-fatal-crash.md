KPA Boss Capt. William Ruto's Daughter Buried Friday After Fatal Crash

The daughter of Kenya Ports Authority (KPA) boss Captain William Ruto is set for burial this Friday, following a fatal road accident that claimed her life and injured several family members.

Mitchell Jelimo Kemboi, daughter to the recently reappointed KPA Managing Director Captain William Ruto, died on Friday evening after a tragic road accident in Taita Taveta. The crash also saw other family members hospitalised, though KPA has confirmed they are in stable condition and receiving medical care. The authority is cooperating with investigations into the accident.
