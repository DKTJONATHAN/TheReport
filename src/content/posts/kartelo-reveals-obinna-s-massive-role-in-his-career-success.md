Kartelo Says Obinna Played "Big Role" In His Career. Yeah, Right.

Another comedian, Kartelo, is out here singing praises for Oga Obinna, claiming the guy was instrumental to his career. Apparently, it was all about "mutual respect" and "maturity." Let's be real, in Nairobi, that usually translates to someone using you until they don't need you anymore, or maybe just until they get their own cut. It's always about what you can do for them, isn't it?
