Wueh, welcome to Kenya, where the ink on the last ballot paper hasn-t even dried before the vultures start circling the next one. We are more than 500 days ahead of schedule, yet the country has already shifted into full campaign mode. Forget about the 2022 pledges that are currently gathering dust in government offices; those were just fairy tales told to get the keys to the treasury. Now, the roadside addresses and strategic defections are back, because governing is hard, but shouting from a sunroof is easy.

Kalonzo and Rigathi are out here complaining that the President-s recent by-election "clean sweep" was just him competing against himself. It is a bit rich coming from men who have spent their lives in the same system. Ruto, on the other hand, is busy confirming that UDA and ODM will "compete" for every seat. It is a beautiful piece of theatre, really. They want us to focus on their staged rivalry while the price of unga stays in the clouds and the youth remain jobless. It is just another version of [Nanyuki-s Badminton Circus: Another -Youth Empowerment- Scam](/posts/nanyuki-s-badminton-circus--another--youth-empowerment--scam) but on a national scale.

Then we have Senator Cherargei claiming that detectives have "detailed files" on "goon networks." Please. In this city, we know how those files work. They appear when a politician needs to be silenced and vanish when a deal is struck. It is the same old script we see in [THE POLICE JUSTICE CIRCUS: STEP RIGHT UP AND WATCH THE FILES DISAPPEAR](/posts/the-police-justice-circus--step-right-up-and-watch-the-files-disappear). These files aren-t for justice; they are for leverage. They don-t catch the criminals; they just negotiate the price of their freedom.

The IEBC is also trying to look busy by proposing restrictions on campaign funding sources. It is laughable. In a country where money moves in sacks and "donations" are just bribes with better branding, who are they kidding? They want to track the money while the elite have already mastered the art of hiding it. It is just another layer of bureaucracy that will be bypassed by anyone with the right connections.

So, prepare yourselves for 500 days of noise, dust, and empty promises. The roads will be blocked by motorcades, and your ears will be filled with rhetoric about "the people" from men who wouldn-t recognize a commoner if they sat on one. The 2027 race hasn-t "quietly roared to life" - it has lunged at our wallets. If you thought the last few years were tough, just wait until you see how much it costs to fund a three-year campaign on a bankrupt budget.
