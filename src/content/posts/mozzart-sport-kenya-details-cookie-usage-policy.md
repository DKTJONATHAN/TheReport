Mozzart Sport Kenya has detailed its use of cookies on its platform. The company states that cookies are employed to enhance user experience and improve service functionality.

The cookies used are categorised into two main types. Mandatory cookies are essential for ensuring the proper operation of the website.

Additionally, the platform utilises "other cookies." These include analytics cookies, which help in understanding site usage, cookies for facilitating the sharing of page content on social networks, and cookies designed for displaying targeted advertisements and content to users.

Users have the ability to manage their cookie preferences. This control can be exercised by selecting the 'Set cookies' option, which is located at the bottom of the page.
