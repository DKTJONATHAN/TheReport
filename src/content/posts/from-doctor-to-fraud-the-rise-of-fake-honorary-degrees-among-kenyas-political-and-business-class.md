# From 'Doctor' to Fraud: The Rise of Fake Honorary Degrees Among Kenya's Political and Business Class

I get genuinely furious every single time another Kenyan politician or shady businessman announces their shiny new "Doctorate" title. On a scale of 1 to 10, my anger hits 11. Why? Because most of us actually went to school, sat through boring lectures, wrote painful exams, and earned our qualifications legitimately only to watch illiterate clowns in suits parade fake PhDs while running our country into the ground. These honorary degree scams have become so normalized that nobody even blinks anymore when Oscar Sudi suddenly becomes "Eng. Dr." overnight.

Let me paint the picture. Your MP posts a graduation photo on social media wearing oversized gown and mortarboard. They struggle through broken Swahili speech that sounds like primary school dropout level. The "university" turns out to be some unaccredited degree mill operating from WhatsApp groups or hotel conference rooms. Commission for University Education (CUE) issues warnings that nobody follows up on. State House allegedly pulls strings to kill investigations. And voters? Many celebrate the "achievement." This is Kenya's new reality.

## Oscar Sudi: The Clown In Academic Robes

Oscar Sudi takes the cake for ridiculousness. The Kapseret MP proudly received "honorary doctorate in leadership and management" from Northwestern Christian University in December 2024. CUE immediately launched probe because that institution lacks accreditation and barely exists physically. Sudi celebrated by posting videos of himself struggling through broken Swahili speeches that prove he never progressed beyond Class 3 English level. 

This same MP literally never picks up microphone in parliament for actual debate. He disappears during serious legislative work but materializes instantly for fake graduation ceremonies. Now he calls himself "Eng. Dr. Oscar Sudi" on every platform while actual engineers laugh quietly. How does someone who cannot construct coherent English sentence suddenly become "Doctor of Leadership"? Pure comedy if it was not so damaging to Kenya's global reputation.

## Sakaja's Never-Ending Degree Court Saga

Johnson Sakaja represents the blueprint for degree fraud survival. Nairobi Governor faced multiple court cases questioning Team University degree validity during 2022 campaigns. IEBC cleared him narrowly but questions persist about whether that Ugandan institution actually delivered legitimate education. Sakaja wears "Professor" title proudly despite academic controversy hanging over his head like dark cloud.

Sakaja mastered art of academic laundering perfectly. Flood courts with lawyers. Pressure regulators through political connections. Keep smiling for cameras while wearing graduation gown. Voters eventually forget details and celebrate "Professor Sakaja" running their city. Pattern repeats endlessly across Kenya's political class.

## John Waluke And The Northwestern Degree Factory

Sirisia MP John Waluke joined Oscar Sudi receiving identical fake honorary PhD from same unaccredited Northwestern Christian University. Both MPs paraded certificates during same ceremony raising immediate red flags about organized degree-selling operation targeting gullible politicians. CUE probe followed but predictably stalled under political pressure.

Waluke represents classic case of politician needing academic legitimacy fast ahead of elections. Pay degree mill operators substantial fee. Receive instant "Doctorate." Display proudly during campaigns. Hope voters never dig deeper into accreditation details. Exact same script plays repeatedly.

## Mombasa Hotel PhD Scandal Exposed

Mombasa delivered Kenya's most comical degree fraud in 2025 when unknown "university" awarded multiple PhDs after just three days "training" at Whitesands Hotel conference room. Politicians and businessmen lined up paying fees for instant doctorates while actual professors watched in disbelief. Event looked exactly like cheap wedding reception except participants wore graduation gowns.

CUE issued strong warnings against such blatant degree mills but enforcement remains nonexistent. Hotel conference room transforms into "university campus" for weekend. Politicians fly in from across Kenya. Certificates printed on spot. Social media photos go viral. Nobody questions accreditation until next scandal erupts.

## CUE: All Bark, No Bite Against Powerful

Commission for University Education (CUE) maintains list of 15+ unaccredited institutions selling fake degrees to politicians. Northwestern Christian University tops list. Team University follows closely. Various Mombasa hotel "universities" occupy multiple slots. CUE issues regular warnings, launches occasional probes, publishes blacklists.

Reality remains brutally different. Powerful politicians enjoy complete protection. State House reportedly intervenes directly blocking de-accreditation processes. System protects itself ruthlessly.
