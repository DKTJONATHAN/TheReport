Honey, if you are surprised that your in-laws are trying to hijack your wedding, you clearly have not been paying attention to how these family contracts work. People spend thousands on a single day just to realize they have signed up for a lifetime of meddling. It is the same kind of delusion that keeps the gossip industry running - people chasing a fairy tale only to realize they are trapped in a low-budget sitcom.
