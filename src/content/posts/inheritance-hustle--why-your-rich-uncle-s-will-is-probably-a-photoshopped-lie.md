In Nairobi, we don't just bury the dead; we bury the truth along with them. The moment a tycoon stops breathing, the family tree turns into a pack of hyenas. It doesn't matter if you were a former president or a real estate mogul with half of the CBD in your pocket. Your "final word" is only as final as the best forgery money can buy in a River Road back alley.

The courts in Eldoret and Mombasa are currently choked with the drama of the wealthy. Take the Sogomo family, where Sh400m in Treasury bonds and prime land are being treated like loose change at a bus park. Chang'masa Sogomo and Kiprotich AK Chesire are being accused of forging documents to grab the loot. It is the same old script: the body is barely cold, but the "will" has already been edited more times than a social media influencer's selfie.

This level of greed shouldn't surprise anyone who lives in this city. We are used to being robbed by professionals. Whether it is the taxman or your own sister Gabrielle, the goal is the same: take what isn't yours. We know that [your payslip is a crime scene, and the government is holding the knife](/posts/your-payslip-is-a-crime-scene--and-the-government-is-holding-the-knife), so why should we expect a multi-million-shilling inheritance to be any different? Fraud is the national anthem, and the wealthy just sing it in a higher key.

Now the widow is begging the DPP and DCI to step in, hoping that the law can fix a family that has already rotted from the inside. She is looking for justice in a system where files disappear faster than a politician after an election. From nine vehicles to buildings in the CBD, the stakes are high enough to make brothers forget they share the same DNA.

At the end of the day, these grand estates are just monuments to human greed. The "intended beneficiaries" are usually just the ones who were too slow to hire a better lawyer or a more creative document "specialist." If you think your family is different, just wait until there is a title deed on the table. In Nairobi, love is a luxury, but a forged signature is a career path.
