**PASTOR EZEKIEL ISSUES STAGED APOLOGY TO BAHATI AFTER WILD ORGAN HARVESTING LIES EMERGE FROM CHURCH**

So, Pastor Ezekiel Odero, the man with the mega-church and the even bigger media presence, has done the honourable thing. He's issued a public apology to Bahati, the musician, because one of his sheep decided to go rogue and start slinging mud. Apparently, this congregant, in the hallowed halls of New Life Church no less, decided to accuse Bahati of organ harvesting and killings. Because, you know, that’s what you do in church - accuse people of being modern-day vampires.

This whole circus kicked off when some footage went viral. Imagine this: a live broadcast, a pastor, and then this random person spewing unsubstantiated garbage about Bahati being part of some shadowy organ-harvesting ring. The church, it seems, is not just a place for spiritual upliftment anymore; it's also a prime spot for character assassination. It’s almost as if some people in leadership are oblivious to the damage they can cause, much like the chaos surrounding [Pastor Kanyari’s church was demolished](/posts/pastor-kanyari-s-church-was-demolished).

Bahati, understandably, is not amused. He's out here denying every bit of it, calling the accusations a complete fabrication. And he’s not just going to sit there and take it; he’s talking legal action. Good for him. It’s about time someone stood up and said enough is enough to these unfounded, explosive claims being made in places that are supposed to be sacred.

Pastor Ezekiel, bless his media-savvy soul, has now stepped in to clean up the mess. He’s made a public apology, broadcast live, no doubt to show everyone how seriously he’s taking this. The solemn tone, the formal broadcast - it all screams "we messed up, please don't sue us." It's a classic case of damage control, trying to salvage both Bahati's reputation and the church's image, all while reminding us that sometimes, even in the most spiritual of settings, things can go very, very wrong.
