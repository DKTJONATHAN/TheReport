WARNING: More foreign money, more foreign control. Nairobi's getting another layer of "development" from Seoul, but don't expect it to fix anything overnight. Just more paperwork and promises.

They say it's about boosting investment and infrastructure, but let's be real, it's about them having a bigger stake in our backyard.
