Justified Accord 2026 is here, and 1,500 soldiers are currently running around the bushes of East Africa pretending that a few drills will stop al-Shabaab. It is the same old script: the US Africa Command brings the toys, and Kenya and Tanzania provide the soil. They call it "operational readiness," but we all know it is just a high-stakes photo op to make us feel safe while the real threats are sitting in air-conditioned offices in Nairobi.

While the military is "sharpening crisis response," the Orange Democratic Movement is having a real-time crisis of its own. With Raila Odinga gone, the party is behaving like a headless chicken. Oburu Oginga has finally moved against Edwin Sifuna, proving that in Kenyan politics, loyalty is a currency that devalues faster than the shilling. This internal collapse is just another example of [The Silent Exodus: The Geopolitical Erosion of the African Marriage](/posts/the-silent-exodus-the-geopolitical-erosion-of-the-african-marriage) where even our political unions cannot survive the weight of ego and greed.

Then we have the Ministry of Health, which has managed to "lose" 11 billion shillings from the Social Health Authority in just six months. That is not just a loss; it is an Olympic-level feat of theft. They transitioned us from NHIF to SHA just to make the looting more efficient. While you are waiting in line at a public hospital with no medicine, remember that someone just bought a fleet of cars with your "social health" contributions.

The Kenya Pipeline Company is busy talking about transporting refined products to Uganda, but the only thing being refined here is the art of the squeeze. They talk about "regional integration" and "maritime security" as if these things put food on the table for a mama mboga in Kibera. It is all a smokescreen. The soldiers drill, the politicians fight over Raila’s ghost, and the money disappears into thin air.

We are a country of "strategic hubs" and "multinational frameworks," but we cannot even keep a secretary-general in a party or 11 billion in a health fund. Justified Accord is the perfect name for this mess, because the elites feel perfectly justified in taking everything while we watch the parade. Same circus, different year.
