While central bankers pretend that micro-adjustments to interest rates can tame the beast of oil-driven inflation, the reality is far more grim. Higher energy costs are a tax on the poor and a death sentence for manufacturing-heavy economies like Japan. The Yen is already buckling under the weight of import costs, proving that even the most advanced nations are just one supply chain disruption away from irrelevance. The Australian dollar might gain on hawkish expectations, but that is merely a temporary reprieve for a country whose survival depends on shipping dirt to a slowing China.

In India, the facade of a clean corporate expansion is crumbling. The US indictment of Adani exposes the rot beneath the "India Inc" branding, threatening to choke off the very overseas fundraising that keeps the engine running. At the same time, Donald Trump's recycled obsession with H-1B visas is turning the professional aspirations of millions into a geopolitical bargaining chip. It is a cynical game where human talent is treated as a liability rather than an asset, forcing a generation of skilled workers to live in a state of perpetual anxiety.

China is not waiting for permission to leave the burning building. By selling dollar bonds in Saudi Arabia, Beijing is signaling the beginning of the end for Western financial hegemony. This is a direct assault on the US-led order, much like Huawei's attempt to build a digital fortress with its own operating system. They are no longer trying to play by the rules; they are trying to burn the rulebook before the West uses it to finish them off.

This global posturing is reminiscent of how Ruto Plays Global Main Character While Iran Slides Into the Mentions With a Reality Check (/posts/ruto-plays-global-main-character-while-iran-slides-into-the-mentions-with-a-reality-check). Everyone is auditioning for a role that no longer exists in a world that is rapidly fragmenting into hostile silos. The momentum stocks and market experts are merely providing the background music for a collapse that has already begun. There are no safe havens left, only different degrees of exposure to the coming storm.
