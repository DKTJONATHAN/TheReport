Two Arrested, 20 Slaughtered Donkeys Found in Makindu - Nairobi Gets Another Scare

Authorities in Makindu have busted a donkey slaughter ring just as it was about to flood Nairobi with dodgy meat. Two people are cooling their heels while the rest of the syndicate, no doubt with pockets lined, have vanished into the bushes. This is just another day in our glorious republic, where the only thing guaranteed is that someone's trying to pull a fast one.
