He's talking about "growing hostility" as if it's a surprise, but honestly, who's really shocked? After all, promises are cheap, especially when you're dealing with the same old political circus. He's now hoping God will sort out this mess, because clearly, his own efforts haven't exactly been a masterclass in political strategy.

Everyone's making promises, and we all know how those usually turn out. Remember the Sh642 million debt the State pledged to assume? Let's just say that money doesn't grow on trees, and someone, somewhere, will eventually have to pay.
